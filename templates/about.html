About Us

Codemonkey is a website to teach elementary and middle school students programming languages. With the exponential rise of computers over the past few years, this knowledge is invaluable in the computer science field.

The course is created by Daniel Tomov, a high school senior with a passion in computer science. He noticed that many online programming courses do not teach programming very well. They do not teach it like they were explaining it to a first grader. Codemonkey is made to change that. With a targeted demographic of elementary and middle school students, Codemonkey is a perfect fit for anyone who wants to get started in computer science!
